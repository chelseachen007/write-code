Let's Work Together

I've been known to work on websites for people from time to time. If you've come here and you're thinking about hiring me for a project you have in mind, first let me say to you that I'm flattered that you would consider me. Your project is very important to you, and I respect that.

First, let's talk about what I do so that we can be sure that I'm a good fit.

Web performance consulting

Maybe your website has been kind of dogging it lately. This is my favorite kind of problem to work on (I did write a book on it, after all.) If you're interested in talking about what I can do to make your website faster for your visitors, please do get in touch.

Building HTML templates

You might have some Photoshop files that you need built into HTML templates. I love doing this kind of work. I do it quickly, with great accuracy, and with performance in mind.

On-site consulting

Do you need me to come to your place? I can probably do that. Let's talk about the particulars, and see if an on-site consultation is a good fit for your organization.
